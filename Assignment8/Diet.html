Diet and Supplements

Diet is a huge part to any form of heath, lifting or not, it decides most things in our bodies. having a good diet is the start to all things exercise. Though you don't have to give up all the good foods you've come to know and love over the years, you can just dial it back a little and maybe every once and a while substitute that cheat meal with something a little healthier. I am a huge believer that you shouldn't have to eat just rice and chicken every meal to feel and function healthy. The average person isn't going to training in the olympics. We just want to feel healthy and just overall better about the control we have over our mind and body. I also think that most supplements are kind of scamming. I'm sure there are some out there that really do work, but other than whey protein, creatine, and maybe something like amino acids, they are probably a waste of your time. Even creatine, it can be super effective in some people and others will have little to no reaction. It's all about trial and error and finding out what works best for you. Just remember that it takes time and the greatest part about exercise isn't the gains or losses of weight/muscle, it's simply the fact that you're out and moving, trying new things and learning new things about yourself.
